Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2025

Welcome to VaultLocker. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and secure your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Information: Username, Email ID, Phone Number.
  • Stored Passwords: All passwords are encrypted for security.
  • Authentication Data: OTP, login attempts, session information.
  • Profile Information: Profile picture and user settings.
  • Settings Preferences: Theme selection (Light/Dark mode).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To securely store and encrypt your passwords.
  • To verify your identity through authentication and OTP verification.
  • To allow users to reset passwords securely.
  • To customize theme settings and user preferences.
  • To prevent unauthorized access and enhance security.

3. Data Security Measures

  • Encryption: All stored passwords are securely encrypted.
  • Authentication Checks: Password decryption requires user authentication.
  • Limited Password Visibility: Decrypted passwords are shown only for 10 seconds.
  • OTP-Based Login: Additional security via One-Time Password verification.
  • Session Management: Auto-logout after periods of inactivity.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Access

VaultLocker does not share or sell user data to third parties. Your personal information is only accessible to you through secure authentication.

5. User Rights & Controls

  • Access and modify information
  • Delete stored passwords
  • Customize theme settings
  • Request account deletion

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

VaultLocker reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy. Users will be notified of significant changes through their registered email.
